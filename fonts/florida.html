    First stop: Tallahassee

    With plans to start my trip on June 10th, I received a phone call from my good friend Matt on June 9th, to see if I wanted to go Deep Sea Fishing on Friday the 10th. Stoked with the new plans, I rushed to pack up my gear into my car and left late that night to travel to Tallahassee. We woke up early and spent almost 12 hours out on the water. It was a beautiful flat day on the Gulf of Mexico. We caught a few Groupers and Spanish Mackerels along with a couple of black seabass. It was a great day, and an even better jumpstart to my travels! Thanks, Matt and John for a fun day on the boat!

    The next day I met my good fiend Ken for breakfast and then began my drive to New Orleans!

    See ya' later Florida!
