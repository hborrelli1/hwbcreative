Florida

Let the roadtrip adventure begin!!

For years I have dreamt about take a roadtrip around the country to adventure, explore, and look for a the perfect place to live. After saving up for a year (thanks Mom and Dad for letting me stay rent free!), it was finally time to start my cross-country roadtrip adventure. As of now, I have about 4 months of travel planned out. My general route will be:

FL > LA > TX > NM > AZ > CA > HI > OR > WA > VBC > UT > CO.

After that its up to fate!

First stop: Tallahhasse

With plans to start my trip on June 10th, I received a phone call from a good friend on June 9th, to see if I wanted to go Deep Sea Fishing on Friday the 10th. Stoked with the new plans, I rushed to pack up my gear into my car and left that night to Tallahassee. We woke up early and spent almost 12 hours out on the water. It was a beautiful flat day on the ocean. We caught a few Groupers and spanish mackerals along with a couple black seabass. It was a great day, and and even better jumpstart to my travels!

The next day I met a good fiend for breakfast and then begin my drive to New Orleans!

See ya' later Florida!
