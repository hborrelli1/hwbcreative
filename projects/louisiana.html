Louisiana

Down in the Bayou.

After leaving Tallahassee, my next destination was New Orleans! Rich with culture and history, this city has much to offer. From some of the best Oysters i've ever had to the craziness of Bourbon Street, New Orleans was definetly a good time! I rode my bike along the Levee that follows along side the Mississippi River. This trail offers a nice scenic view.

I was lucky enough to have a personal tour guide and a free place to stay with my cousin in Jefferson, Thanks Iliaura! Although going deep sea fishing cut my time in New Orleans short, it was a lot of fun. Defintely a place I will visit again!

Hacienda Alegra

Having lots of connections makes traveling so much better! I went to visit a friends ranch about an hour north of New Orleans. The ranch named Hacienda Alegria which was a blast! They have a ton of animals at the ranch including horses, mini-horses, goats & mini-goats, an Alpaca, 3 dogs, a bunch of chickens, 3 roosters, a Pot Bellied pig named Luca, and many more! Meeting all the animals, riding ATVs on trails on the property, and getting to experience some awesome cajun food were some of the highlights! Hanging out at the ranch was a blast!
